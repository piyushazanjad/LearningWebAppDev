FAQs

How do I change my password?

  • If you wish to change your password, please follow these steps:
  • Log in to your account
  • Select Hi, Your name at the top right of any xyz.com page
  • Select Profile
  • Select Edit next to the piece of information you wish to update
  • Enter your new information in the appropriate fields
  • Select Save to confirm changes

Can I log in to the xyz.com from any computer with my user name and password, or am I limited to a single computer?
Yes, you can log in to xyz.com from any internet-connected computer. However, you can only be logged in to one computer at a time.

What are the requirements for passwords on xyz.com?
xyz.com works to protect your security and privacy with strong password requirements. These password requirements are part of our certification compliance with TRUSTe’s TRUSTed Websites Program Requirements and the U.S. Department of Commerce‘s U.S.–E.U. Safe Harbor Framework and U.S.–Swiss Safe Harbor Framework. Read our Privacy Policy for more information. Your xyz.com password must:

  • Include at least six characters
  • Include at least one letter and one number
  • Not repeat the same character more than two times in a row
  • Not include white spaces.

How do I change my username Some individual members have the option to choose a unique user name. If you are one of those members and wish to change your user name, please follow these steps:

  • Log in to your account
  • Select Hi, Your name at the top right of any xyz.com page
  • Select Profile
  • Select Edit next to the piece of information you wish to update
  • Enter your new information in the appropriate fields
  • Select Save to confirm changes
